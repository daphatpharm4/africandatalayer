**Segment-Specific Outreach Angle**
- Fintech and payments: `You already win digitally. ADL shows where offline cash-in, cash-out, merchant acceptance, and competitor overlap are weak this week, by cell, before your team spends there.`
- Health and pharmacy: `You do not need another static directory. ADL shows which neighborhoods actually have formal pharmacies, informal competition, and real change on the ground.`
- Retail and agri distribution: `Your constraint is not generic demand. It is outlet density, route reliability, stock visibility, and which corridors are actually workable this month.`
- Mobility, logistics, and addressing: `Map coverage is not delivery truth. ADL verifies passability, landmarks, and route change in the neighborhoods where failure costs money.`
- Advertising and media: `OOH sales teams need inventory truth, not spreadsheet assumptions. ADL can verify panel existence, occupancy, creative turnover, and condition.`
- Partner or channel cohort: `If you already sell transformation or data projects, ADL can become the verified field layer inside your client work instead of another one-off analytics slide.`
