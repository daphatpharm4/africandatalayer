# ADL 6-Week Pilot Plan -- Bonamoussadi, Douala

**Purpose:** Operational field guide for executing the Bonamoussadi pilot from April 14 to May 22, 2026. Covers team structure, budget, KPIs, weekly milestones, and Go/No-Go decision framework. This document is self-contained and designed for day-to-day use by the CEO and Field Team Lead.

**Pilot Dates:** April 14 -- May 22, 2026 (6 weeks, 30 working days)
**Pilot Area:** Bonamoussadi, Douala (geofence: 4.0755--4.0999 N, 9.7185--9.7602 E)
**Verticals:** billboard, alcohol_outlet, census_proxy, fuel_station, mobile_money, pharmacy, transport_road

## 6. Go/No-Go Decision Matrix

**Decision Date:** W6D4 (May 21, 2026)
**Decision Maker:** Charles Mahouve (CEO), with Team Lead input and advisor consultation (Yannick Lefang)

### 6.1 Minimum Success Criteria

ALL 7 criteria must be met for a GO outcome:

| # | Criterion | Minimum Threshold | How Measured |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Total POIs captured | >= 500 | Count of unique CREATE_EVENT records |
| 2 | P0 vertical coverage (transport_road + mobile_money) | >= 70% of estimated POIs | Captured vs. Teammate 1 estimates |
| 3 | Audit accuracy | >= 80% | Spot-check verified POIs matching reality / total audited |
| 4 | Weekly deltas computed | >= 3 (D1, D2, D3) | Computed deltas in database |
| 5 | Agent team functional | >= 2 agents active at pilot end | Active agents on final day |
| 6 | Client outreach initiated | >= 5 prospects contacted | Outreach log count |
| 7 | Platform stability | Zero data loss incidents | Incident log |

### 6.2 Decision Outcomes

| Outcome | Condition | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **STRONG GO** | All 7 met + >= 1 client meeting completed + prospect expresses purchase intent | Expand to full Bonamoussadi + adjacent neighborhoods; hire 2 more agents; target first paid contract within 30 days |
| **CONDITIONAL GO** | All 7 met + outreach sent but no meeting yet OR meeting held but no clear purchase intent | Maintain current team; focus top 3 verticals; intensify outreach for 30 more days; new Go/No-Go at Week 10 |
| **PIVOT** | 5--6 of 7 met OR audit accuracy 70--80% OR agent retention issues | Pause field collection for 2 weeks; fix identified issues; retrain agents; restart; re-evaluate at Week 10 |
| **STOP** | < 5 met OR accuracy < 70% OR data loss incident OR zero commercial interest | Stop pilot; document lessons learned; reassess product-market fit; consider different geography or vertical focus |

### 6.3 What "Good" vs. "Not Good Enough" Looks Like

| Dimension | Strong GO | Conditional GO | Pivot / Stop |
|---|---|---|---|
| Data Volume | 700+ POIs, all 7 verticals | 500--700 POIs, 5+ verticals | < 500 POIs or 3+ verticals missing |
| Data Quality | > 90% audit accuracy, < 5% fraud | 80--90% accuracy, 5--10% fraud | < 80% accuracy or > 10% confirmed fraud |
| Agent Performance | 3/3 active, > 35 subs/day avg | 2/3 active, > 25 subs/day | < 2 active or < 20 subs/day |
| Commercial | 1+ meeting; prospect says "I would pay" | Outreach sent; 1+ positive reply, no meeting | Zero responses or "not interested" |
| Technical | Sync > 99%; zero data loss | Minor sync issues (< 5% failure) | Sync > 5% failure; data loss event |
| Budget | Within 90--100% of plan | 100--115% (within contingency) | > 115% (exceeded contingency) |

## 7. Daily Field Operations Checklist

Print and use daily. Team Lead completes each morning and evening.

### Morning Checklist (8:00 AM WAT)

- [ ] Conduct 15-min standup (WhatsApp voice or in-person)
- [ ] Confirm all agents are present and equipped (phone charged, power bank, data plan active)
- [ ] Assign zones and vertical focus for the day
- [ ] Review any flagged submissions from previous day with agents
- [ ] Remind agents: photo for every submission, GPS must lock before capture, sync before leaving field
- [ ] Send daily standup summary to Charles via WhatsApp:
  ```
  ADL Daily - [Date]
  Agents active: [#/3]
  Yesterday total: [## submissions]
    - Agent 1: ## (Zone [X])
    - Agent 2: ## (Zone [X])
    - Agent 3: ## (Zone [X])
    - Team Lead: ## (Zone [X])
  Quality issues: [none / describe]
  Sync issues: [none / describe]
  Today's plan: [zones/verticals]
  Blockers: [none / describe]
  ```

### During Field Hours (8:30 AM -- 4:30 PM WAT)

- [ ] Team Lead conducts at least 1 spot visit to an agent in the field
- [ ] Team Lead captures own POI submissions (target: 10--15/day)
- [ ] Mid-day check-in via WhatsApp group (12:30 PM): submission count, any issues
- [ ] Agents sync data whenever connectivity allows (WiFi preferred)

### Evening Checklist (5:00 PM WAT)

- [ ] All agents sync remaining submissions before going home
- [ ] Team Lead verifies sync status for each agent in the admin dashboard
- [ ] Agents charge phones and power banks overnight
- [ ] Team Lead notes any equipment issues, agent concerns, or field incidents
- [ ] Team Lead reports end-of-day submission totals to Charles

### Weekly Additions

- [ ] **Friday:** Weekly review meeting at 4:00 PM WAT (45--60 min video call with Charles)
- [ ] **Friday:** Charles triggers weekly snapshot freeze (or verify cron ran)
- [ ] **W3--W6 Fridays:** Team Lead completes spot-check audit (10--50 POIs per week)
