# ADL General Public Pitch Deck

Audience: General public in Douala/Cameroon (residents, local stakeholders, media)  
Goal: Build public trust, explain social value, and encourage participation/support.

## Slide 1
- Slide #: 1
- Title: A Better Local Map for Everyday Decisions
- Core message: ADL helps communities see what is changing around them in real time.
- Slide copy: ADL starts in Bonamoussadi, a neighborhood with an estimated `~560` registered businesses [SM-02], inside a fast-growing Douala metro context of `~4.35M` people [SM-01]. The goal is simple: improve local visibility of essential services.
- Proof points:
  - Douala population estimate [SM-01].
  - Bonamoussadi business-density estimate [SM-02].
- Suggested visual: Community-centered city map with Bonamoussadi highlighted.
- Audience-specific CTA: "Follow the project and share local updates responsibly."

## Slide 2
- Slide #: 2
- Title: Why This Matters for Communities
- Core message: Many official and commercial data sources are outdated or incomplete.
- Slide copy: Cameroon’s last official census was in `2005` [SM-04], while urban growth has changed neighborhood realities substantially [SM-04]. Informal-sector activity is also large (`30-50%` GDP estimate) [SM-03], meaning many important services are not captured well in conventional systems.
- Proof points:
  - Census recency gap and growth context [SM-04].
  - Informal economy scale [SM-03].
- Suggested visual: Historical timeline showing data-age gap.
- Audience-specific CTA: "Support better local data so public and private decisions can improve."

- Proof points:
  - Informal pharmacy context [SM-09].
  - Road infrastructure constraints [SM-05].
  - Mobile-money market structure [SM-08].
- Suggested visual: Three impact lanes: health, transport, financial access.
- Audience-specific CTA: "Use and share verified information, not rumors or stale maps."

- Proof points:
  - Law timeline and compliance context [SM-10][SM-11].
