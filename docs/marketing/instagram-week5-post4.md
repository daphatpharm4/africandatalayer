## Concept

First Saturday post-launch. Quiet, observational, written from street level. Five slides treat the audience as someone walking the quartier on a Saturday evening — what they see, what becomes data, what stays the quartier's. No celebration, no metrics, no founder voice. Mirrors the visual restraint of wk4-post4 ("Built with the quartier") but pivots from gratitude to attention.

**Why carousel:** Sequence reads like a slow walk. One observation per slide, one resolution at the end. Sat-evening audience saves and shares short declarative lines.

**Why now:** Saturday closes wk5 standard cadence. Sets up wk6 without leaning on launch language. Holds the warm tone the brand needs after a launch-week of structural posts.
