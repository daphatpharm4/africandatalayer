## Concept

Second drop of Fun Fact Friday locks the format with a fact most non-Cameroonians underestimate and most Cameroonians take for granted: in Douala, finding a mobile money point on a typical block is faster than finding a bank branch. The agent network is denser, more distributed, and more dynamic than the formal banking footprint — which is precisely why static directories miss it.

**Why this fact:** Cultural truth, non-controversial, instantly relatable in-country, eye-opening abroad. Ties cleanly to the ADL mobile money vertical (kiosk locations + active hours = volatile data → ground-truth use case). Per FFF series roadmap in `instagram-week2-post3.md`, #02 was pre-locked to mobile money density.

**Why carousel:** Same payoff structure as FFF #01 — hook, setup, why, why-it-matters, why-static-maps-miss-it, CTA. Six slides lets the cultural payoff breathe and maximizes save signal. Carousel re-serve sustains reach past the Friday lunch window.

**Claim posture:** Strictly qualitative. No densities, no ratios, no operator-internal stats. "On most blocks", "more present than", "in many quartiers" — observational language only, per content rules.
