## Concept

First IG carousel after launch week. The story this week is not "we launched" — that ran Mon-Sat already. The story is "the work didn't end, it began." Five slides reframe launch as the starting line and re-center the map as the actual milestone. No celebration confetti, no founder voice, no metrics flex. Closes with a soft, evergreen DM CTA so the post keeps pulling new agent interest into the week.

**Why carousel:** Reflective beat works best as paced sequence — one declarative line at a time. Carousel re-serve also reaches the audience that scrolled past launch week noise. Each slide is a screenshot-and-share line.

**Why now:** Tuesday is the first standard IG slot post-launch. Sets the tone for wk5 cadence (Thu agent slot + Fri FFF #04). Holds momentum without leaning on the launch hashtag for a fifth day in a row.
