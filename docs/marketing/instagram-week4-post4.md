## Concept

Launch week closer. Three jobs: thank the people who carried W1–W3 (without naming or showing real agents), reframe the launch as the start of the work not the finish, and leave a soft download nudge for anyone who waited until the dust settled. Five slides — short, declarative, unified terra-and-navy palette. No celebration confetti, no founder selfie, no metrics flex.

**Why carousel:** Sequence reads as a letter — "to the quartier, from us." Each slide is one sentence the audience could screenshot and share alone. Carousel re-serve also captures the Sunday-morning save audience that misses Saturday-evening posts.

**Why now:** Saturday closes launch week before LinkedIn milestone is the only Friday signal. IG community post is the warmer mirror — same gratitude, no metric language. Sets up W5 cadence reset (back to standard Tue/Thu/Sat IG schedule, FFF #04 the following Friday).
