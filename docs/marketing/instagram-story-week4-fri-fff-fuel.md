## Concept

Three-frame echo amplifies Friday's fuel-price Fun Fact. Frame 1 is a clean visual hook tied to the carousel headline. Frame 2 quizzes the audience on whether Douala pump prices are uniform — converts the carousel's claim into a bet, locks recall. Frame 3 harvests candidates for FFF #04 via a question sticker — turns audience into the editorial pipeline for next week.

**Why story:** FFF posts thrive on the bet-and-reveal loop. Quiz on frame 2 makes the reveal personal — audience commits an answer and then learns whether they were right. Question on frame 3 is the cheapest content-research tool we have for keeping the FFF series fresh without burning a feed slot. Three frames is intentional — keeps tap-completion rate high on a Friday lunch slot when attention is already split.
