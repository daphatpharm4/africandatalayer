## Concept

Week 4 closes on a community frame — the launch isn't the platform shipping, it's the people who built it being named back. Story sequence does three jobs: re-cut the visual hook from the carousel (frame 1), give viewers a screenshot-worthy thank-you tile (frame 2), open a question harvest for next week's quartier-spotlight content (frame 3), and convert any late-week stragglers with a final install link (frame 4).

**Why story:** Saturday evening is the highest-share window on IG — community-frame content lives on shares. Frame 2 is built to be screenshotted (no sticker, full-bleed thank-you) — this is the share asset. Frame 3 turns the W4 community close into the W5 content seed without burning a feed slot. Frame 4 is the "if you waited, the door is open" closer for late-arriving install traffic.
