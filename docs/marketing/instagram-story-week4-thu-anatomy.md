## Concept

Story sequence converts the 6-slide pipeline carousel into a tap-test. Frame 1 hooks with the headline question. Frame 2 polls which gate surprised the viewer most тАФ turns the carousel into a measurable trust audit. Frame 3 quizzes the gate count to lock retention. Frame 4 opens a question sticker harvesting "what else should we verify?" тАФ content seed for W5 trust posts and a low-friction reply path for analyst/buyer-segment viewers.

**Why story:** Trust-narrative carousels live on save rate but die on first-tap depth. Stories drive depth тАФ the poll on frame 2 forces engagement with the gate language, which deepens recall. Question sticker on frame 4 doubles as content research without burning a feed slot. Pinning the trust frames to a separate "VERIFIED" highlight builds a permanent trust shelf on profile.
