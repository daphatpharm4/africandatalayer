## Concept

The launch carousel said "verification pipeline." This carousel shows it. Six slides break a single submission into the five gates it crosses before it becomes a verified data point: GPS lock, photo + EXIF, duplicate check, risk score, trust-tier weight. The payoff slide names what the user finally sees on the map and why that earns trust.

**Why carousel:** A pipeline reads as a sequence — one gate per slide is the natural rhythm. Single-image "infographic of the pipeline" compresses every gate into illegible icons; carousel lets each gate own a frame and a one-line explanation. Save signal also rewards educational carousels.

**Why now:** Wed Twitter/X plan-row asks the same question in 280 chars; IG carries the long-form trust narrative. Trust is the launch-week wedge — new downloaders need to believe the data before they engage as agents or clients. Locks the visual identity from Mon launch (gold stamp swap → `LAUNCH · WEEK 4`).
