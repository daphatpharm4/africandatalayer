## Concept

Launch carousel. Three job: announce, prove, convert. Slide 1 lands the news — ADL is now on the App Store and Play Store. Slides 2–4 earn the download by showing what landed (mobile capture, offline queue, verified pipeline) without screenshots of real submissions or real agents. Slide 5 hands off the CTA: download + DM keyword for support.

**Why carousel:** Launch posts get re-served by the IG algorithm to non-swipers when the format is carousel — single-image launch posts tend to peak at first-hour reach and decay. Carousel also lets the proof slides (offline queue, verification chain) breathe so the headline isn't just "we shipped."

**Why now:** Mon all-platform launch row per `docs/marketing/social-media-launch-plan.md`. IG normally posts Tue/Thu/Sat — Monday is an override for launch synchronization across LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and IG. Carousel locks the launch visual identity (gold `LAUNCH · WEEK 4` stamp) reused across the rest of the week.
