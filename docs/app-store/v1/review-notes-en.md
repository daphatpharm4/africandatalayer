## Accounts for Review
- **Collector:** Provided in App Store Connect secure field
- **Reviewer:** Provided in App Store Connect secure field

## Review Steps
1. Fresh install — sign in with collector credentials
2. View operation overview (map placeholder)
3. Capture a record — form fields + GPS + photo
4. View pending work status
5. Enable airplane mode — verify "Pending" status (no signup/create account)
6. Sign out — verify no session persistence without credentials
7. Sign in with reviewer — verify evidence review access
8. Sign in with administrator — verify member/project management
